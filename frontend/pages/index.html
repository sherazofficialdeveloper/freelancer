FAQ Help

Common Platform Questions

How does double-sided escrow lock work?

When an employer hires a freelancer, they fund the contract value immediately to our secure escrow platform cache. The funds lock and cannot be retrieved arbitrarily. Once the freelancer delivers files and the client confirms, the funds instantly credit to the freelancer's wallet.

Is there a platform fee for job contracts?

Skillnest charges a standard flat 5% administrative fee on completed releases, which covers server-side Socket.io integrations, alert routing, and disputes resolution arbitration support.

How are revision requests processed?

If the deliverables submitted in the "Submit Deliverables" dashboard form do not match client guidelines, the employer can click "Request Revision" within their contract card. This keeps the funds safely locked inside the escrow until the deliverables are corrected.
