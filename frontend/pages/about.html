Protecting the Freelancing Lifecycle

Traditional freelancing boards suffer from payment inconsistencies, uncompleted milestones, and platform fees. Skillnest introduces an elegant, double-sided escrow contract engine inspired by GrapeTask to eliminate disputes.

1

Up-Front Escrow Lock

When an employer accepts a bid and hires, the project budget is instantly locked. This guarantees that the server contains the agreed amount for the freelancer's work, removing the risk of non-payment.

2

Interactive Deliverables Filing

When the workspace milestone is reached, freelancers use the dashboard to submit their deliverables. Files, repositories, design links, and textual descriptions are formally uploaded to the contract card.

3

Milestone Clearance

The employer gains immediate visibility of the assets, evaluates if the criteria are fully met, and presses "Approve & Release Payment" to trigger the escrow dispatch block. Funds are credited to the freelancer's wallet instantly.
