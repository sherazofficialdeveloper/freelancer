Have queries regarding secure escrow accounts, milestone project approvals, and creative bids? Find quick answers below.

How does the secure budget escrow system work?
When a buyer hires a freelancer, the listed project budget is immediately deducted from the buyer's balance and locked inside a secure project escrow contract. The freelancer can work with 100% confidence knowing the funds are guaranteed. Upon delivery of work, the buyer approves the submissions to release escrow credits directly into the freelancer's wallet.
Can freelancers place custom rates on gig proposals?
Yes! Freelancers browse the public job space and place competitive proposals. Inside the proposal, the freelancer designates their customized bidding rate and expectations notes, which are routed to the buyer panel instantaneously.
Are direct chats encrypted and instantaneous?
Absolutely. Direct messaging on Farelancer is powered by robust Socket.io WebSockets, carrying instant chat threads, activity telemetry logs, active typing states, and notifications back and forth instantly without manual page refreshing.
How do I fund my wallet for hiring or cash out completed earnings?
Our sandbox wallet mimics real-world banking ledgers. Buyers receive complimentary credit upon register setup to test and post gigs. Completed freelancers can request mock withdrawals via wire transfer or credit card directly in the secure wallet dashboard.

Marketplace Terms of Service

Last modified: June 8, 2026

1. Platform Scope

Farelancer provides elite software-as-a-service matching services between employers (buyers) and creative freelancers. Users are registered as self-determined contracts and bound under escrow agreements. We do not act as direct employees.

2. Financial Escrow Guarantees

Escrow actions are handled automatically under backend database security. When a buyer recruits, they warrant the budget holds are immediately locked inside secure non-refundable escrow. Approval releases the credit instantaneously. Freelancers must deliver authentic and fully functional assets.

3. Professional Accountability

Any forms of copyright infringement, credential misrepresentation, spam bidding, or malicious behavioral attacks are strictly prohibited and result in immediate ban action by the admin monitoring systems.

4. Privacy Protections

Your private data, encrypted digests, balance ledgers, and transaction metrics are stored using robust database encryption layouts, and never sold to third-party commercial entities.
